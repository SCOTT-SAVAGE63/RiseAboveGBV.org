Our Mission

Our mission is to create a society free from gender-based violence and femicide, where all individuals are treated with dignity, respect, and equality. We strive to prevent GBVF, support survivors, and promote a culture of justice, accountability, and inclusivity, empowering communities to rise together against violence and build a brighter future for all.

Our Journey

2025

Foundation

Rise Above GBVF was founded by a group of concerned citizens in response to alarming rates of gender-based violence and femicide in Shastripark, Phoenix, KwaZulu Natal.
